Affordable Housing: George's 10 Years

  • OCT, 2008: George joins the board of affordable housing society
  • JULY, 2012: Galiano Green bylaws receive 1 st reading of Bylaws 233 and 234
  • SEPT, 2014: Bylaws receive 2 nd reading
  • OCT, 2014: After 6 years and expenditures of $161,000 on studies, consultants, engineers, lawyers and fees, a request for a Public Hearing was denied
  • OCT, 2014: In protest George steps down from the Board and declares his intention to run on a platform to bring the Galiano Green application to a Public Hearing
  • NOV, 2014: George wins the election and immediately begins work with staff and Trustees to move the application forward
  • OCT, 2016: Public Hearing held
  • NOV, 2016: Bylaws read a 3 rd time.
  • JAN, 2017: Approved by the Executive Committee of the Islands Trust to allow 20 densities of affordable housing at Galiano Green
  • APR, 2017: Approved by the Minister of Community, Sport and Cultural Development
  • JUL, 2017: LTC Adopts bylaws 233 and 234
  • OCT, 2018: DPA 1 – Riparian Areas & DPA 5 – Sensitive Ecosystem approved, the final conditions to complete the 10 year saga to bring the first affordable housing project to fruition since 1975.
